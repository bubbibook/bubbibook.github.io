ABOUT BUBBI BOOK
Bubbi Book is a digital publication devoted to showcasing the work of
up-and-coming bubbis. Curated by Joe Kelly and designed by Erika Jaquez, Bubbi Book is a constantly evolving digital creative space.

Open to constant suggestion from a growing pool of contributors, Bubbi Book provides a forum for work that is friendly, fresh, and undiscovered - be it writing, drawing, photography, illustration, animation, music, or whatever else.
